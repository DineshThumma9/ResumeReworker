# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users
Job seekers and professionals who need to tailor their resumes for specific job descriptions and applicant tracking systems (ATS). Their context is task-oriented, high-focus, and timeline-sensitive, requiring high confidence that their original resume details are preserved while alignment is optimized.

## Product Purpose
Resume Reworker is an intelligent agentic workspace that automates resume tailorship and LaTeX typesetting. It eliminates the manual friction of tweaking experience points, analyzing gaps, and recompiling PDF templates on the fly, delivering a fast, reliable, and ATS-compliant output.

## Positioning
Safe, agentic resume tailoring that tailors experience points for role alignment through self-correcting loops without fabricating details.

## Brand Personality
- **Voice & Tone:** Precise, objective, reassuring, and expert-confident.
- **Visual References:** The minimal utility and layout discipline of Linear, Stripe, and Vercel, combined with the high-readability text containers and clean paper layouts of Notion and GitBook.
- **Emotional Goals:** Professional reassurance, control, and utility-focused focus.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, generic AI wrappers covered in loud gradient texts, glow effects, floating glassmorphic cards, and heavy decorative drop shadows.
- Dry, unstyled enterprise templates or flat table layouts that lack visual polish and look like generic administrative panels.
- Loose consumer landing pages with giant fluid hero headings and low text density.

## Design Principles
1. **Preserve Source Truth:** Visual accents and suggestions must never distract from the candidate's authentic data. Design for high information trust.
2. **Clean Spatial Rhythm:** Avoid nested card layouts. Separate distinct sections with generous space, while keeping related log items, bullet metrics, and summaries grouped tightly.
3. **High-Density Legibility:** Enable comfortable side-by-side workspace comparison. Optimize typography hierarchies, tracking, and spacing so dense lists are easy to scan and read in long passages.
4. **Continuous Live Feedback:** Demystify the backend agent loop. Stream granular, formatted state progress chronologically so the user is always in sync with compiling and judging actions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target WCAG AA compliance (minimum 4.5:1 text-to-background contrast ratio for standard body text, and 3:1 for large/bold text).
- Explicit light/dark mode neutral scaling to ensure reading comfort in different environments.
- Support responsive viewport breakpoints to scale the side-by-side layout cleanly onto smaller display screens.
